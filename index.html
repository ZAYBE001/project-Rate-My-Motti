Rate My Motti

Rate My Motti is a website that allows users to rate the mottos of others. Users can submit their own mottos and have them rated by other users. The website will display the top rated mottos on the homepage.

Users can also search for mottos by keyword or category. They can also view the mottos of a specific user.

Rate My Motti is a fun and interactive way to share your personal motto with others and see how it compares to others.
